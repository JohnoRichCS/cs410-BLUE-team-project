Faíct - The Fact AI Checking Tool

Real-time, cross-platform fact checking for text, audio, video, and AI-generated content.

Abstract

  • Misinformation spreads rapidly, especially with the rise of AI-generated content.
  • Current tools are slow, limited in scope, and not designed to effectively support human verification.
  • Faíct verifies information in real time across text, audio, and video.
  • The system provides credibility assessments, cited sources, and AI-generated content detection.
  • Faíct helps users quickly identify misinformation and make informed decisions.

The Problem

  • Internet users are exposed to rising levels of misinformation from online sources and platforms.
  • AI-generated posts, articles, and social media accounts make misinformation harder to detect.
  • Content verification tools are often limited in scope and accessibility.
  • Users lack a universal, on-demand method for quickly verifying information.

Who Is Most Affected?

  • Journalists, content creators, and moderators who rely on real-time content verification.
  • Academic institutions, research facilities, and risk management teams with strict validity requirements.
  • Students, researchers, and everyday users following news, politics, podcasts, or social media discussions.

Solution

  • Cross-platform fact checking across text, links, and social media posts.
  • Real-time speech-to-text fact checking for podcasts, videos, and live conversations.
  • Video-to-transcript processing for uploaded or linked videos.
  • AI-generated content detection across text, images, and video.
  • Credibility scoring supported by trusted sources and clear explanations.
  • Designed to assist and speed up human fact-checking, not replace it.

What Makes Faíct Different?

  • Combines fact checking, transcription, video analysis, and AI detection in one tool.
  • Supports more than text-based verification.
  • Works across multiple platforms instead of being limited to one website or app.
  • Provides fast, evidence-backed credibility insights on demand.

Monetization Plan

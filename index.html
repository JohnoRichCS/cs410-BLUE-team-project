Faíct - The Fact AI Checking Tool

Real-time, cross-platform fact checking for text, audio, video, and AI-generated content.

Elevator Pitch

Misinformation spreads rapidly across social media and online platforms, especially with the rise of AI-generated content. Current tools are often slow, limited, and not designed to support real-time verification.

Faíct enables users to verify information across text, audio, and video by extracting claims and analyzing them using AI and trusted sources, providing credibility scores and evidence.

Problem

  • Misinformation is increasing across online platforms
  • AI-generated content makes false information harder to detect
  • Tools are limited to specific platforms or topics
  • Manual verification is time-consuming

Customers & End Users

  • Journalists and content creators
  • Students and researchers
  • Social media users
  • Organizations requiring accurate information

Solution

  • Cross-platform fact checking (text, links, posts)
  • Real-time speech-to-text fact checking
  • Video-to-transcript verification
  • AI-generated content detection
  • Evidence-based credibility scoring

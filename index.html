Faíct - The AI-Assisted Fact Checking Tool

Real-time, cross-platform fact checking for text, audio, video, and AI-generated content.

Elevator Pitch

Misinformation spreads rapidly across social media, news platforms, and online communities, especially with the rise of AI-generated content. Current fact-checking tools are often slow, limited in scope, and mostly focused on text verification.

Faíct helps users verify information in real time across text, audio, and video by extracting claims and analyzing them against reliable sources. The system provides credibility assessments, supporting evidence, and AI-generated content detection.

Problem

  • Misinformation is increasing across online platforms.
  • AI-generated content makes false information harder to detect.
  • Current tools are often platform-specific, slow, or limited to viral content.
  • Manual verification is time-consuming and often skipped.

Customers & End Users

  • Journalists and content creators verifying claims quickly.
  • Students and researchers checking source credibility.
  • Moderators reviewing online content.
  • Organizations with strict information validity requirements.
  • Everyday users following news, politics, podcasts, or social media discussions.

Solution

  • Cross-platform fact checking across text, links, and social media posts.
  • Real-time speech-to-text fact checking for live audio.
  • Video-to-transcript processing for uploaded or linked videos.
  • AI-generated content detection across text, images, and video.
  • Evidence-based credibility scoring with supporting sources.
  • Designed to assist and speed up human verification, not replace it.

What Makes Faíct Different

  • Combines fact checking, transcription, video analysis, and AI detection in one tool.
  • Supports more than text-based verification.
  • Works across different platforms instead of being locked to one website.
  • Provides fast, on-demand credibility insights with evidence.

Glossary

AI: Artificial Intelligence.

API: Application Programming Interface.

FAICT: The Fact AI Checking Tool.

GUI: Graphical User Interface.

IDE: Integrated Development Environment.

LLM: Large Language Model.

AI-Generated Content: Text, images, audio, or video created or heavily modified by artificial intelligence tools.

Browser Extension: A small software add-on that adds features or functionality to a web browser.

Credibility Score: A rating that estimates how trustworthy or reliable a claim appears based on supporting evidence.

Disinformation: False or misleading information that is intentionally created or shared to deceive people.

Fact-Checking: The process of verifying whether a claim is accurate by comparing it against reliable sources.

Misinformation: False or misleading information that may be shared regardless of whether there is intent to deceive.

Speech-to-Text: Technology that converts spoken audio into written text.

Trusted Sources: Reliable references, databases, publications, or organizations used to verify claims.

Verification: The process of confirming whether information is accurate, reliable, or supported by evidence.

Video-to-Transcript: The process of converting spoken content from a video into written text for analysis.
